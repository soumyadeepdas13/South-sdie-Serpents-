THE LAWS OF SERPENTS

#1

1. No serpent stands alone & A serpent never shows cowardice.

2. If a serpent is killed or imprisoned, their family will be taken care of.

THE LAWS OF SERPENTS

#2

3. A serpent never sheds its own skin.

4. No serpent is left for dead.

THE LAWS OF SERPENTS

#3

5. A serpent never betrays its own.

6. In unity, there is strength.

7. An Extra Law:A serpent should be willing to die for his own kind.
